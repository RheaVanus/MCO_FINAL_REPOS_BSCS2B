SAGAYAN the Fierce Warrior Dance of Maguindanao

Originates

Nestled in the core of Mindanao, in the Philippines, Sagayan is a repeatedly performed dance of warriors that represents the indomitable character and gallantry of the Maguindanao tribe. The tribal dance has been continuously taught to succeeding generations, being the result of a fusion of folk customs, Islam, and tribal myths.

History

Sagayan is a classic Maguindanao dance that carries the community's cultural heritage through warrior-like movements, which are a fusion of Islamic and indigenous traditions. Its rituals, attire, and choreography are a reflection of the fusion of cultures, and they also transmit the tales and morals from one generation to the next.

Warrior Dance of Bravery

The performance known as Sagayan, which is the Warrior Dance of Bravery, is a representation of the unfearing nature of the Maguindanao fighters. This traditional dance reflects the war shouting of the earlier times, creating an atmosphere of undaunted military spirit. The dance is a demonstration of the Maguindanao fighters' determined bravery and valor, who were the real heroes behind this magnificent performance.
