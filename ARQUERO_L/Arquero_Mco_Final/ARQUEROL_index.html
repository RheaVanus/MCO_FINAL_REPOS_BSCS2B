RUMBA DANCE

Rumba is a sensual, slow-paced Latin ballroom dance originating from Afro-Cuban folk dance, known for its hip action and intimate partner connection. It is often called the "dance of love" and is danced to slow, romantic music with a steady 4/4 time signature.
