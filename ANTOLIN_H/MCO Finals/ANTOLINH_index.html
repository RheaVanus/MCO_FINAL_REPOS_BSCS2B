Pandanggo sa Ilaw

Originates

Pandanggo sa Ilaw is a Philippine folk dance influenced by the Spanish fandango. The "sa ilaw" version features dancers balancing oil lamps (or glasses) on their heads and hands to showcase balance and poise.

History

The dance blends Spanish influences with local Filipino folk expressions. Traditionally performed at celebrations, it highlights rhythm, balance, and cultural storytelling through movement.
